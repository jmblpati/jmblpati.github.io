I will be on the academic job market for the 2024-2025 cycle.

Research Interests

My research interests lie broadly in continuous optimization, high dimensional probability, and graph algorithms. More specifically, I am interested in the power of continuous iterative techniques to solve combinatorial problems and in the use of combinatorial structure to create algorithms for structured convex optimization.
